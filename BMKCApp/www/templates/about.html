BeerMeKC is the definitive app for beer aficionados in the Kansas City Area. Enter your preferences – beer type, alcohol by volume (ABV), and international bittering units (IBU) and be prepared to send your palate on a wondrous tour. BeerMeKC will present you with a recommended beer. If you like the recommendation – tap “Get It” and we’ll show you how. For those swipers out there: A swipe left means “Nah…not a fan of that one.” A swipe right means “Sounds good, just not right now.” Swipe down if you really don’t like it. Swipe up if you love it and want it to be a top recommendation.

Just so you know, we are an app for those who love a good local beer. If you want watered down, recycled beer – just get on I-70 and head east or west. If that doesn’t apply to you, then go hit BeerMe! Seriously, why are you still reading this…do we need a button on this page for you? Fine…
